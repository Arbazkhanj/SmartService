Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartService. We value the privacy of our visitors, especially Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) who handle customer documentation. This Privacy Policy documents the types of information we do and do not collect and record.

2. No Server File Storage (Important)

SmartService is designed as a browser-sandboxed toolkit. When you upload photos, PDF files, signatures, or government identity certificates (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter card, driving licenses) to edit, print, or compress, these files are processed ENTIRELY inside your local web browser.

Your files are never transmitted to our servers, nor are they shared with any third-party APIs. All operations are rendered on an HTML5 canvas locally on your computer. Once you close the browser tab, the temporary memory buffer is immediately destroyed.

3. Log Data and Analytics

Like other standard portals, we use basic anonymous traffic tracking (such as standard server access logs and Google Analytics) to study search metrics and count how many users visit different tools. This traffic data includes anonymous IP fragments, browser types, and timestamp metrics, none of which can identify you or your customers.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to save user preferences, such as selected tool presets, category selections, or theme parameters (dark/light toggle) to make your next visit faster.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to support@smartservice.com.
