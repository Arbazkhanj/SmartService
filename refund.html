Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

At SmartService, we strive to provide VLEs and portal operators with highly optimized, browser-based utilities for document processing, image resizing, and printing preparation. Since our services are purely digital, we request our users to review our refund and cancellation policy prior to purchasing premium keys or unlocking premium dashboard integrations.

2. Instant Digital Delivery

Please note that all premium tools and VLE dashboard expansions are activated instantly upon successful transaction verification. Since activation is fully automated and credentials or session access are delivered digitally to your browser local storage or email, there is no physical shipping or delivery involved in using SmartService.

3. Refund Eligibility & Cancellation

Due to the irreversible nature of digital license activations and immediate resource access:

  • Successful Tool Activations: Once premium tools have been successfully unlocked and used/accessed by the VLE operator, refunds are not eligible.
  • Technical Activation Failures: In rare cases where your payment was successful but the tool session did not unlock, please reach out to us with the transaction ID. We will resolve the issue by manually unlocking access immediately or, if requested, issue a manual cancellation and full refund.
  • Failed Transactions / Double-Debits: If money was debited from your bank account or wallet, but the transaction timed out or failed before reaching us, the payment gateway will process an auto-refund. This will be automatically credited back to your original payment method.

4. Refund Processing Timelines

In cases where a refund is approved by our support team, the refund process will be initiated. The approved amount will be credited back to your original source of payment (UPI ID, Debit/Credit Card, Bank Account, or Wallet) within 5 to 7 business days.
